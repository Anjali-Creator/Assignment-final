Thave Foundation

Thave foundation is a registered non-governmental organisation based in Bangalore that works in the field of mental health, Health, Environment, socio-Economic and Education. It connects grassrootwork in Community development through various sectors. Thave focus to create awareness about the Mental Health to uphold the basic concept " The state of Physical, Mental and social well-being" and Thave works at community level to understand the needs of communities and applications of basic income to transform lives.
